1 Getting Started

This manual will guide you through the complete process of filling up the Leo Club of Kathmandu Alka nomination form. The form is organized into 6 easy steps with a progress bar at the top to track your progress.

  • 1
    Open Your Browser
    Open any web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari) on your computer or mobile phone.
  • 2
    Visit the Form URL
    Go to: https://ktmalka-nomination.vercel.app
  • 3
    Welcome Modal Appears
    A popup will appear showing the official "Notice & Criteria" images. Read them carefully.
  • 4
    Review Both Slides
    Use the arrow buttons (◂ and ▸) or dots below the image to navigate between the Notice and Criteria slides.
  • 5
    Click "I Understand"
    After reading, click the gold "I Understand" button to close the modal and access the nomination form.
⚠️ Warning
Make sure you read the Notice and Criteria carefully before proceeding. They contain important eligibility requirements and deadlines.

2 Member Directory Search

The floating "🔍 Search Directory" button on the top-right of the form opens a sidebar drawer where you can search for your member details and auto-fill the form.

  • 1
    Click the Search Button
    Look for the floating "🔍 Search Directory" button at the top-right corner of the page.
  • 2
    Sidebar Opens
    A sidebar drawer slides in from the right side of the screen.
  • 3
    Type Your Search
    Enter your name, email, or phone number in the search box.
  • 4
    View Results
    Matching member records will appear below the search box with name, Leo ID, email, and contact details.
  • 5
    Select Your Record
    Click on your member record to auto-fill your Leo ID and personal details into the form.
  • 6
    Close the Sidebar
    Click the ✕ button or click outside the sidebar to close it.
💡 Tip
This feature is optional. If you already know your Leo ID, you can directly enter it in Step 1. The search is only available if you have been added to the member database by the admin.

4 Step 2: Position Selection

Select the position you are applying for from the dropdown menu. The form will display the eligibility requirements and nomination fee for the selected position.

  • 1
    Open the Dropdown
    Click on the "Position Applying For" dropdown to see all available positions.

Executive Positions:

President  •  Vice President  •  Secretary  •  Treasurer

Board Positions:

Board of Director  •  Joint Treasurer  •  Joint Secretary  •  Tamer  •  Tail Twister  •  IT Committee  •  Signature Project Committee  •  Leo Membership Chairperson  •  Public Relation Officer

Each position has specific eligibility requirements and nomination fees. Executive positions generally have higher fees.
  • 2
    Select Your Position
    Click on the position you wish to apply for from the dropdown list.
  • 3
    Review Eligibility & Fee
    A panel will appear showing the eligibility requirements and the nomination fee for your selected position.
  • 4
    Click "Next Step →"
    Proceed to the next step after reviewing the requirements.
⚠️ Warning
Make sure you meet all the eligibility requirements before selecting a position. The criteria are set by the admin and may change each tenure.

5 Step 3: Candidate Statements

In this step, you will write about your experience, interests, and future plans. These statements help the selection committee understand your qualifications and vision for the role.

  • 1
    Past Experience & Learning
    Describe your past experience and what you have learned as a member of the Leo Club. Include specific activities, projects, or events you have participated in.
  • 2
    Areas of Interest
    Share your areas of interest such as community service, leadership development, IT, design, public speaking, event management, etc.
  • 3
    Future Plans for the Position
    Outline your key goals and plans for the position you are applying for. Be specific about what you intend to achieve and how you plan to contribute.
  • 4
    Click "Next Step →"
    Proceed to the document upload step.
💡 Tip
Write thoughtful and genuine responses. These statements are reviewed by the election/selection committee. Be specific with examples rather than writing generic statements.

6 Step 4: Required Documents

Upload all required documents in the accepted file formats. Each file has a maximum size limit of 5 MB. Make sure your documents are clear and readable.

Required Documents & Accepted Formats:
Document Accepted Format Max Size
Cover Letter (Goals & Vision) PDF only 5 MB
Formal Photo JPG only 5 MB
Copy of Citizenship PDF only 5 MB
Club Dues Paid Receipt PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP 5 MB
Candidate Signature JPG, PNG, WEBP 5 MB
  • 1
    Click "Choose File"
    For each document, click the "Choose File" button to open a file browser dialog on your computer.
  • 2
    Select Your File
    Navigate to the file on your device and select it. The file name will appear next to the button.
  • 3
    Verify File Format
    Make sure each file matches the accepted format listed in the table above. Invalid files will be rejected.
  • 4
    Upload All 5 Documents
    All 5 documents are required. The form will not proceed if any document is missing.
  • 5
    Click "Next Step →"
    Once all files are uploaded, proceed to the payment step.
⚠️ Important
Cover Letter must be in PDF format. Formal Photo must be in JPG format. Citizenship must be in PDF format. Double-check your file types before uploading!
💡 Tip
If your file is too large (over 5 MB), compress it using online tools like ilovepdf.com (for PDFs) or tinypng.com / tinyjpg.com (for images).

7 Step 5: Payment Details

Pay the nomination fee using eSewa by scanning the QR code displayed on the form. After payment, upload the receipt screenshot and enter your transaction code.

  • 1
    Note the Fee Amount
    The nomination fee is displayed prominently on the page. This amount was determined by the position you selected in Step 2.
  • 2
    Open eSewa App on Your Phone
    Open the eSewa mobile application on your phone.
  • 3
    Scan the QR Code
    Use eSewa's "Scan & Pay" feature to scan the QR code shown on the nomination form.
  • 4
    Complete Payment
    Enter the exact nomination fee amount in eSewa and complete the payment.
  • 5
    Save the Receipt
    Take a screenshot of the payment confirmation receipt showing the transaction code.
  • 6
    Upload Receipt Screenshot
    Click "Choose File" under "Nomination Fee Paid Receipt" and upload your payment screenshot.
  • 7
    Enter Transaction Code
    Type the transaction code or reference ID from your eSewa receipt into the "Payment Transaction Code" field.
  • 8
    Click "Next Step →"
    Proceed to the final declaration step.
⚠️ Important
Keep your eSewa transaction receipt safe. The admin will verify your payment transaction code before approving your nomination.

8 Step 6: Declaration & Submission

This is the final step. You must agree to the declaration statements, review your application in the preview modal, and then confirm your submission.

  • 1
    Criminal Record Declaration
    Check the box confirming: "I confirm I have no criminal record."
  • 2
    Terms & Conditions Agreement
    Check the box confirming: "I agree to the general terms and conditions and declare that all details filled above by me are correct and true."
  • 3
    Click "Submit Nomination"
    Click the green "Submit Nomination" button at the bottom of the form.
Preview & Confirm:
  • 4
    Preview Modal Appears
    A preview popup will display all your entered details for a final review, including your name, position, fee, uploaded documents, and statements.
  • 5
    Review Carefully
    Scroll through the preview and verify every field is correct.
  • 6
    "Edit Details" or "Confirm & Submit"
    Click "Edit Details" to go back and make changes, or click "Confirm & Submit" to finalize your nomination.
  • 7
    🎉 Congratulations!
    If the submission is successful, a congratulations popup will appear with your submission summary. Click "Done" to close.
ℹ️ Note
Once you submit, your nomination status will be "Pending" until the admin reviews it. You cannot edit your submission after it is submitted.

9 Troubleshooting & FAQ

Q: The page shows "Nominations Closed". What should I do?
The submission deadline has passed. Contact the admin for more information or check if the deadline has been extended.
Q: My Leo ID is not being verified. What went wrong?
Make sure you are entering the correct Leo ID. If the issue persists, use the "Search Directory" button to look up your member record, or select "No" for Leo ID and enter your details manually.
Q: I can't upload my file. It says "invalid format".
Check the accepted format for each document: Cover Letter → PDF only, Formal Photo → JPG only, Citizenship → PDF only. Convert your file to the correct format before uploading.
Q: My file is too large (over 5 MB). How do I compress it?
For PDFs, use ilovepdf.com to compress. For images, use tinypng.com or tinyjpg.com to reduce file size while maintaining quality.
Q: I don't have eSewa. How can I pay?
Contact the club admin for alternative payment methods. The form currently supports eSewa QR payment only.
Q: I submitted but made a mistake. Can I edit my submission?
Once submitted, you cannot edit your application. Contact the admin to request changes or corrections to your nomination.
Q: The form is not loading properly on my phone or computer.
Try using a modern browser like Chrome or Edge. Clear your browser cache (press Ctrl + Shift + Delete) and try again. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
Q: Where can I check my nomination status?
Your nomination status will be communicated by the admin. The admin panel shows all submissions with their current status (Pending, Approved, or Rejected).

About Us

About Leo Club of Kathmandu Alka

The Leo Club of Kathmandu Alka is a youth organization sponsored by the Lions Club of Kathmandu Alka. It focuses on community service and personal development for its members.

The Leo Club of Kathmandu Alka was chartered on February 10, 2016. It operates as part of District 325 F, Multiple District 325, Nepal.

Club ID: 126845
Jawalakhel, Lalitpur

Our Vision

To cultivate a community of compassionate and responsible young leaders who are dedicated to serving their communities and creating positive change.

Our Mission

To provide opportunities for young people to develop leadership skills, gain experience in community service, and contribute to the well-being of their local, national, and international communities through various projects focusing on areas such as healthcare, education, environmental protection, and aiding the disadvantaged.

Strategic Priorities of Tenure

Identify Leadership Roles
Collaboration & Partnership
One to One Mentorship
Igniting Passion
